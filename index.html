We create experiences to make you move

We make VR and AR experiencies to improve health. In the medical context, by creating a physical rehabilitation therapy platform. In the personal context, by creating applications and experiences to fight sedentarism and make people move.

We are a technology first startup, our movement detection technology has many applications and has flawless precision, thus enabling the creaton of many different applications.

Phyisical Therapy and Health

Every year, millions all around the globe need to attend some form of physical therapy. This rehabilitation therapy is often times a painful one, and all of the times, a very monotonous and repetitive task.


We are creating a physical therapy platform, leveraging the power of AR and VR technologies to create deeply interactive and meaningful experiencies, and developing our own movement detection systems to track the limbs and articulations of patients, improving their treatment with gamification theory.

Sedentarism and Exercise

In Argentina alone, 40%+ of the population is sedentary. This means walking less than 2 hours per week. The whole planet is in phyisical activity crisis, not aware of its cognitive and health benefits.


We believe this can be situation can be reversed by using VR to create engaging and movement inducing experiences. Our movement detection and classification systems achieve this, by making the user's body the controller. No more buttons, just your body
