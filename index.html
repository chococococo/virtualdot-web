Physical Therapy and Health

Every year, millions around the globe need to attend some form of physical rehabilitation therapy. Sometimes it is a painful one, but all of the times it is a very monotonous and repetitive task.


Adherence is very low, most people never complete rehabilitation treatments, and this leads to lifetime problems.


We are creating a physical therapy platform that uses VR, AR and own movement detection systems to track the limbs and articulations of patients, assisting and correcting them in real time. We want the patient to stop being a patient, and turn into a player.

Sedentarism and Exercise

In the Americas alone, 45%+ of the people are sedentary. This means walking less than 2 hours per week. The whole planet is in physical activity crisis, not aware of its cognitive and health benefits.


We believe this can be reversed by using VR to create engaging and movement inducing experiences. Our movement detection and classification systems achieve this, by making the user's body the controller.
